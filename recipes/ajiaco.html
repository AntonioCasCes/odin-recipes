Ajiaco

By Xemenendura - Own work, CC BY-SA 3.0, wikimedia

Ajiaco is a traditional colombian recipe, which contains several types of potatoes, corn, chicken and others ingredients that give it it's characteristic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 8 water cups
  • 1 big breast, without bones and skin
  • 1 cup of guascas
  • 2 pound of red potatoes, without skin and cut it in big pieces
  • 1 middle onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic tooths
  • 1 pound of potatoes Yukon
  • 3 big corns
  • 1/2 cup of pork crackling
  • salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. On a big pot, add water, the breast and 1/2 cup of guascas. Leave it over low heat about 40 minutes. Once chicken is ready, remove it from the water and place it on a plank. Keep the liquid in the pot, use two forks for separe the breast in pieces of good size. Cover with aluminium foil to heat conserve
  2. Add the chicken breast and leave it here for 45 minutes.
